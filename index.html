FASTQ ↔ FASTA Matcher

Upload the FASTA sequences you expect and the FASTQ reads you sequenced. This runs entirely in your browser — nothing is uploaded anywhere. Two independent methods cross-check each other: k-mer containment, and a k-mer-seeded local aligner producing a MAPQ-like confidence score. If your reads are much longer than the region of interest (e.g. a long amplicon read where only part of it corresponds to a short reference/variant region), lower the containment threshold below — a read that's mostly non-reference flanking sequence will naturally have low containment even for a perfect match in the overlapping part.
